    Asteroid search campaign

    Orbit'21

    Astronomy & Space Science Association of D.S.Senanayake College, Colombo 07 is organizing the Orbit'21 Asteroid Search Campaign from 5th of March to 30th of March 2021, in collaboration with International Astronomical Search Collaboration (IASC) of Hardin-Simmons University at Texas - USA, under the support of the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA). Orbit'21 Asteroid Search Campaign is the first ever Asteroid Search Campaign organized by a school astronomical society in Sri Lanka. You can register from here.

    The Astronomy & Space Science Association of D.S. Senanayake College was founded in 1993. During the course of the past 28 years the Astronomy & Space Science Association of D.S. Senanayake College has been able to organize many meaningful and successful events such as “Orbit”, “Exodes” and “Infinite” and has become one of the leading astronomical societies in Sri Lanka.
